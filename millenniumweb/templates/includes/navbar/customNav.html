  • Porcelain Tiles

    Porcelain tiles or ceramic tiles are porcelain or ceramic tiles commonly used to cover floors and walls, with a water absorption rate of less than 0.5 percent.

  • Wall Tiles

    Tiles are often used to form wall and floor coverings, and can range from simple square tiles to complex or mosaics.
  • Floor Tiles

    Tile is a manufactured piece of hard-wearing material such as ceramic, stone, metal, baked clay, or even glass, generally used for covering roofs, floors, walls, or other objects such as tabletops.

  • Double Charge Tiles

    Double Charge vitrified tiles are fed through a press that prints pattern with a double layer of pigment; they are 3 to 4 mm thicker than compared to other tiles.
  • Full Body Vitrified Tiles

    Soluble salt vitrified tiles are screen printed and polished.

  • Parking Tiles

    Parking Tiles based on terracotta and ivory and Digital Parking Tiles manufactured using inkjet printing technology.
